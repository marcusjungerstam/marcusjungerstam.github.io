    • Bill & Coin

    I had the privilege of contributing to the creation of the intro movie for the slot game 'Bill & Coin'.

    In this project, I did the all the look development, enviroment, enviroment effects/effects, and compositing.

    Fun facts:

    The art director wanted a realistic but still a toone shader look. So I renderd two renders. One with only toon shaders and one with the ai standard shader and mixed them in comp. Didnt't make a huge diffrence but it was a good way to kind of break realism. Most fun to do was the bg Bokhe particles.
