    • Dead Riders Trail

    I had the privilege of contributing to the creation of the intro movie for the slot game 'Dead Riders Trail'.

    In this project, I assumed the roles of lighting, modeling, look development, effects, and compositing.

    Fun facts:

    This was a big project with a lot of shortcuts. Every shot tok around 5 days from start of animation to finished comp. The effects were basically the same set up(in Bifrost) in all shots but with some variations. The trickiest part was to get that wet look without running simulations. In the end I created normal maps that I used to create the water droplets on the character with a camera projection. It was a lot of Maya and Nuke back and forth on this one.
