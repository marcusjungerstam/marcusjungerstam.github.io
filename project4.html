    • Net Gains

    I had the privilege of contributing to the creation of the intro movie for the slot game 'Net Gains'.

    In this project, I assumed the roles of lighting, look development, effects, and compositing.

    Fun facts:

    Even though this short intro is a one shot intro it was quite challenging. The cached fur on the character was around 50gb of data. And the longest rendered frame(frame 90)tok 95 minutes on a rtx3090 to render. Doesn't seem like a shot that's hard to render but fuzzy fur is a nightmare when it comes to AA samples.
